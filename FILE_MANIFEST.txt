🔧 CONFIGURATION NEEDED
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. MONGODB CONNECTION (REQUIRED):
   Edit: backend/.env
   Add your MongoDB URI:
   MONGODB_URI=mongodb://localhost:27017/lost-found
   
   OR for MongoDB Atlas:
   MONGODB_URI=mongodb+srv://user:pass@cluster.mongodb.net/lost-found

2. OPTIONAL CUSTOMIZATION:
   - Change colors in frontend/css/styles.css
   - Update college name in HTML files
   - Modify contact info in footer
